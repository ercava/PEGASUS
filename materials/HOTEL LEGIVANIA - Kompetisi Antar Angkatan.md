# LEGIONNAIRE 2024 (Hotel Legivania) — LinkedIn Experience Copy (by Position)

**Event context used across all entries:** Legionnaire 2024 ("Hotel Legivania"), a Hotel Transylvania-themed inter-class sports, literacy, and arts & culture tournament initiated by Bidang 3 of OS Cendekia (Divisi Olahraga, Divisi Sastra & Budaya, Divisi Akademik Literasi), OSIS MAN Insan Cendekia Serpong. Held 9–15 June 2024 across the MAN Insan Cendekia Serpong campus, open to the full grade 10 and 11 student body, spanning 12 competition events, an 11-person BPH plus an 8-division organizing committee, and a total managed budget of Rp13,496,800.
