# SONIC LINGUISTIC 2025 — LinkedIn Experience Copy
*BPH (Core Committee) & Event Organizer roles*

Ready-to-use LinkedIn "Experience" entries for the core committee (BPH) and the four Event Organizers of **SONIC LINGUISTIC 2025 "Celestial Conquest,"** the flagship national student competition run by OSIS MAN Insan Cendekia Serpong. Swap in your name, copy the title and bullets into your profile, and trim to 3 bullets if you want it shorter — all figures below are pulled directly from the official closing report and workload analysis, so they'll hold up if anyone asks.

**Shared facts used throughout:**
- National-scale event: 16 competition branches across 4 fields (Science & Technology, Language, Journalistic, Sport/Art/Culture), targeting 2,500 student participants from across Indonesia.
- 3-day on-site finale (17–19 April 2025) at MAN Insan Cendekia Serpong, capping an 11-month project that began in June 2024.
- Rp 387.4 million mobilized across 9 funding streams; Rp 352.6 million spent, closing with a Rp 34.8 million surplus.
- Lineup redesigned from 19 to 16 competitions — retiring 3 legacy events and launching a brand-new STEM Competition.
- Delivered under a new no-guest-star policy, shifting the sponsorship pitch toward jury credibility and program curation instead of celebrity draw.
