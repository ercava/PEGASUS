**Event context used across all entries:** Festival Bahasa dan Kewirausahaan (FBK) 2024, an Encanto-inspired Language Month celebration and entrepreneurship festival spanning Sastra Budaya, Akademik Literasi, Olahraga, and Kewirausahaan (Market Day, Workshop, Teater) divisions, organized by OSIS MAN Insan Cendekia Serpong.
